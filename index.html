DOCTORS CHARITY

At Doctors Charity, we provide pro bono medical services to break down barriers to healthcare access. Our team offers home visits, distributes medical equipment, and ensures quality care for those with limited mobility. Beyond local efforts, our global medical missions bring vital healthcare to underserved communities, ensuring everyone receives the care they deserve.

Our Mission

Every person deserves access to life-improving and lifesaving information, medical care, and economic opportunities to support their families. We are dedicated to providing medical care and resources to the economically disadvantaged. Our mission is to deploy effective and coordinated efforts to ensure people gain access to quality medical care and economic resources.

Join Us Via Telemedicine

Doctors Charity uses information technology to connect qualified healthcare workers who wish to volunteer their time and skills to alleviate the health issues that plague the continent of Africa with the people who need such services. For volunteers unable to leave their jobs to travel for onsite medical intervention, telemedicine offers the perfect avenue to contribute. Working within their free time and schedules, Doctors Charity can arrange consultation appointments between these volunteers and patients in Africa, efficiently achieving the same goals. Ihotu Telemedicine Clinic in Nigeria is a major partner in this initiative.
