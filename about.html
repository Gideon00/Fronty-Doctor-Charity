We are Doctors Charity

Empowering Doctors, Transforming Lives: Bridging the Healthcare Gap We connect skilled healthcare workers with communities in need, making a lasting impact.

Who We Are

Established in 2008 by a team of visionary doctors, Doctors Charity is a multi-service nonprofit based in New York, helping people worldwide. Currently, we operate in the U.S. and Africa to deliver medical care and equipment, health and education, mindfulness, and investment in agriculture to communities that need it most. We have six major projects reaching hundreds of people in need.

Building a Legacy of Service

Our ultimate aim is to build a legacy of service. We envision a day when medical professionals worldwide will set aside one day each month from their busy schedules to volunteer their time, providing free and quality healthcare in poor communities. We are about service—service to the people and service to the community.

We envision a world where medical professionals and other healthcare providers make service to their people, their communities, and neighboring communities an integral part of their monthly schedule.

We believe that by coming together, we can build a global community of medical care where we all share responsibility for each other’s well-being. We envision a world where no one goes bankrupt due to illness or dies from lack of care caused by poverty.

Our Mission

Every person deserves access to life-improving and lifesaving information, medical care, and economic opportunities to support their families. We are dedicated to providing medical care and resources to the economically disadvantaged. Our mission is to deploy effective and coordinated efforts to ensure people gain access to quality medical care and economic resources.

Who We Work With

Doctors Charity partners with projects to assist medical professionals in delivering medical care, equipment, supplies, and medical training in the U.S. and the developing world. We invite doctors and other professionals interested in joining youth communities to volunteer for any of our projects or donate funds to support the deployment of these key initiatives. There are many ways to give back, and we have focused on these core areas:

Service: Delivering pro bono medical services through home visits and medical missions abroad.

Resource Distribution: Providing medical equipment to communities in need.

Financing: Rallying funds to support microfinancing and farming initiatives.

Our Partners

You can also partner with us by donating to any of our projects today. Additionally, you can donate medical equipment to support underserved communities in West Africa.
